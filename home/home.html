About Us

With over 20 years of award-winning design, we are an established industry leader in Austin who is recognized nationwide. Our integrated approach ensures our clients are heard, partners are involved, and details are carefully managed.

Established in 2002, Britt Design Group was started by Laura Britt as a wellness-focused interior design firm. As we have grown, we have expanded our focus to incorporate art curation, sustainability in processes and materials, and a fully integrated approach to crafting unique interiors. We specialize in luxury residential, hospitality, and commercial spaces ranging from private estates to mixed-use developments. We are a collective of designers with diverse backgrounds, united by our drive to create curated experiences that foster joy for our clients and ourselves.
